01 · Leads & Conversions
Opportunity Registration & Lead Intake
Opportunities are detected and registered using a unique identifier that flows CRM → ERP, creating a traceable thread from first contact through delivery. The SLD is received manually via email — the first friction point in an otherwise digital intake flow.

Key challenge: The CRM-to-ERP handoff was manual and error-prone. No structured intake for SLD data — a gap I mapped as a priority input for the digital suite team.
◆ Solution I Ideated & Vetted
Built by Saichandar Brahadeesh — a full end-to-end audit of a connected-product manufacturing workflow (lead → delivery), mapping every manual vs. digital handoff across 8 stages and 7 functional teams. The "Solution Layer" toggle overlays AI-pilot concepts I personally ideated and, in two cases, took to vendor proof-of-concept. Anonymized to protect employer-confidential process details.
